# "Our Distance" — Helios — Listener Reactions

## Which track this is (disambiguation)

"Our Distance" is track 3 (5:23) on Helios's 2020 album **Domicile** (Ghostly
International; released Dec 11, 2020). Helios = **Keith Kenniff** (Pacific NW
composer; also records neoclassical as **Goldmund**; wife **Hollie Kenniff**
collaborates on Helios). The context-seed guess "Our Distance and That of Trees"
does **not** appear to be a real Helios title — the actual track is simply "Our
Distance." Namesakes to avoid: the Fray's album *Helios*, Symphonium *Helios*
headphones, and the band Flights of Helios — none related.

[Ghostly artist page](https://ghostly.com/artists/helios),
[Keith Kenniff — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Keith_Kenniff).

## Synthesis

Ordinary-listener reaction to *this specific track* is sparse online (no YouTube
comment text extractable, empty Last.fm shoutbox, RYM/AOTY gated). What exists is
